Description

The aim was to be able to be aware of the system health and status. The solution was quite obvious. Recommend and deploy monitoring solution. After doing a comparison research, decision was made to utilize Zabbix, as it has out-of-the box support of used systems, LDAP connector, zoom-in-zoom-out charts, auto-discovery and customization abilites. Afterall, SysAdm team was now alerted of any system failures via different channels (e.g. mail or SMS depending on the incident severity), nice dashboard could be presented to the Service Desk team, and SLA reports for Service Managers.
